Hope Community Rapid Re‑Housing Program

Located in Grand Rapids, Michigan, Hope Community is dedicated to meeting the transitional housing needs of low-income women and their minor dependent children. Applicants are referred by The SalvationÂ Army.

Our facilities include a Service Center that features twelve private apartments as well as twelve duplex units. As residents gain the skills they need to progress toward independent living, they receive a Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher that empowers them to find private rental housing in our community.

Hope Community housing facilities were built by members of the Home & Building Association of Greater Grand Rapids, who donated both labor and materials. The program is administered by Grand Rapids Housing Commission and is supported by a HUD Rapid Re-Housing Program grant.

Because grant funding does not cover many basic necessities, the need for goods and services is ongoing; we continue to rely on the generosity of community donors to obtain many essential supplies and services.
